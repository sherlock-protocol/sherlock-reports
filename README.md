<h1 class="center" style="color:red"> About Sherlock </h1>

Sherlock is the first and only smart contract auditor to repay protocol teams with up to $10M in the event of a missed vulnerability. Sherlock is also the first and only auditor to combine the best of the legacy audit model (designated senior security expert on each project) with an audit contest model (independent auditors competing head-to-head). This approach ensures that some of the top security experts in the space are assigned to your codebase and incentivizes dozens of other security experts to compete for the most high-severity findings.
